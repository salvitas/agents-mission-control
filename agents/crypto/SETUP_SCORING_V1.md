# Setup Scoring v1.1 (1–10)

## Score Components
1. Regime alignment (0–2)
2. Trend quality / structure (0–2)
3. Catalyst strength (0–2)
4. Liquidity / execution quality (0–1)
5. Risk-reward quality (0–2)
6. Correlation/portfolio fit (0–1)

Total: 0–10

## Interpretation
- 8.5–10: A-tier (can size near max allowed)
- 7.0–8.0: B-tier (standard size)
- 6.0–6.9: C-tier (half-size / optional)
- <6.0: pass

## Minimum Requirements
- R:R must be >= 2.0 to first meaningful target
- Clear invalidation level (no invalidation = no trade)
- No major event risk inside next hour unless event-play setup

## Position Sizing Guardrails
- A-tier: up to 1.0% risk
- B-tier: 0.5–0.75% risk
- C-tier: 0.25–0.5% risk

## Hard Stops
- 2 consecutive losses in same session -> pause 30 mins
- 3 losses/day -> stop trading for day
