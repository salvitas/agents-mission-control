# Ethereum Scaling and Rollup Economics

**Domain:** blockchain
**Date:** 2026-03-16
**Research status:** pending_review

## Executive Summary
Rollups are delivering throughput, but economics remain unsettled as fee markets, sequencing models, and interoperability patterns continue to evolve. The core debate has moved from scalability to value capture.

## Key Findings
- Data availability improvements lower user fees but can compress margins for individual rollup operators. _[confidence: medium]_
- Fragmented liquidity across rollups still imposes friction and MEV externalities on users. _[confidence: medium]_
- Shared sequencing and intent-based routing are emerging as attempts to reduce UX and security complexity. _[confidence: medium]_
