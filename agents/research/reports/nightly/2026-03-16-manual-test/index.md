> One-off manual pipeline test output. All items remain pending review (no LinkedIn drafts generated).

## Top Takeaways
- Adoption narratives are broadening, but execution risk is still underpriced across most domains.
- Regulatory and standards clarity is increasingly a growth enabler, not just a constraint.
- Operational maturity (security, observability, governance) now differentiates winners more than headline innovation speed.
- Interoperability and integration debt are common bottlenecks from finance to software tooling.
- Confidence should be updated frequently; several themes still show structurally unresolved contradictions.
