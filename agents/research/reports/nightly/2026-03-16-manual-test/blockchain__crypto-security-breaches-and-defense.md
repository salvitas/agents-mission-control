# Crypto Security Breaches and Defense

**Domain:** blockchain
**Date:** 2026-03-16
**Research status:** pending_review

## Executive Summary
Attack surfaces are expanding faster than defensive maturity, especially around bridges, privileged keys, and social engineering. Security posture is increasingly determined by operational discipline, not just smart-contract audits.

## Key Findings
- Most large losses still involve key compromise, governance capture, or integration flaws rather than novel cryptography breaks. _[confidence: medium]_
- Bug bounty programs and formal verification improve detection but do not address organizational response delays. _[confidence: medium]_
- Insurance and proof-of-reserves disclosures are becoming competitive trust signals for users and partners. _[confidence: medium]_
