# AI Policy, Copyright, and Liability

**Domain:** ai
**Date:** 2026-03-16
**Research status:** pending_review

## Executive Summary
The regulatory center of gravity is moving toward accountability for model deployment and data provenance, not just model training disclosures. Litigation pressure is now a product strategy variable.
