# Frontier Model Releases and Benchmarks

**Domain:** ai
**Date:** 2026-03-16

## Executive Summary
Frontier model competition is accelerating, but benchmark gains are becoming less informative without cost, latency, and reliability context. Buyers are shifting toward capability-per-dollar and deployment safety metrics.
