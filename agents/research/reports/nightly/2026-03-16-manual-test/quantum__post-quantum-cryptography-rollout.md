# Post-Quantum Cryptography Rollout

**Domain:** quantum
**Date:** 2026-03-16
**Research status:** pending_review

## Executive Summary
PQC adoption is progressing in standards and pilot deployments, but enterprise migration is constrained by crypto inventory gaps and legacy system dependencies. The implementation challenge is organizational as much as technical.
